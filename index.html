An interactive exploration of global wellbeing across 147 countries
Francisco Cardoso — Powered by Claude · D3.js·Python / pandas

The World Happiness Report is an annual global survey ranking 147 countries by how citizens rate their own lives. Published by the Oxford Wellbeing Research Centre using Gallup World Poll data, it breaks happiness scores into six factors: GDP per capita, social support, healthy life expectancy, freedom, generosity, and perceptions of corruption.

Every year I explore this report, drawn to understanding what truly shapes a good life. Here you can see where your country ranks, compare how each factor contributes, and highlight regional patterns.
